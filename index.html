Our Promise to You
✅ We NEVER sell your data
✅ We NEVER share your data with anyone
✅ We NEVER show ads using your data
✅ You can delete your data anytime
Data Storage
Your financial data is stored with bank-level AES-256 encryption on Supabase servers located in Mumbai, India. Your data never leaves India.

Only you can access your data through your personal login. VKV Coder staff do not access, view or process your personal financial information.
What We Store (On YOUR Behalf)
• Your name and email — for login only
• Financial data YOU enter — stored securely for YOUR access only
• Payment confirmation ID — only to activate your plan

We do NOT collect, read or process your personal financial data.
Your data is stored FOR YOU, not BY US.

We do NOT collect your location, contacts, photos or any device information.

🔒 Think of us as a bank locker —
We hold it safely, but only YOU have the key!
Your Rights
• Access: All your data is visible in the app
• Export: Download your data as CSV/PDF anytime
• Delete: Request complete deletion anytime
• Portability: Your data belongs to you

For any support, queries or account deletion, contact: vkvcoder.support@gmail.com
Security
• AES-256 encryption at rest
• TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
• Row Level Security — each user sees only their own data
• No third party analytics or tracking
